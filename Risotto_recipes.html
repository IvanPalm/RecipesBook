Risotto radicchio and gorgonzola

Info

Time
45 mins
Difficulty
Easy
Servings
4

Ingredients

350 g
carnaroli rice
2 or 3
tablespoon olive oil
~100 g
red onions
~200 g
radicchio
1 liter
broth with carrots, celery, and potatoes
120 g
gorgonzola cheese
1
tablespoon honey
1
glass milk
1
apple
1
tablespoon walnuts
1
tablespoon raisings
2
glasses of white wine (one is to drink while cooking!)

Directions

  1. Prepare the broth by boiling two or three carrots, celery, two potatoes and parsely in water with a little salt
  2. Slice the red onion finely (make the blade wet before slicing, if your eyes are sensitive to onions. Just saying!)
  3. Remove the central white part of radicchio with a knife, then cut the rest in slices of about one cm width
  4. Pour olive oil on a deep pan, make sure it's big enough to hold also the rice later
  5. Put the pan on gentle fire and fry the onions slowly, until they become golden
  6. Add the sliced radicchio and cover with a lid
  7. Blend together gorgonzola, honey and milk with a fork or a blender
  8. Crush the walnuts in relatively small pieces
  9. When the radicchio is soft, remove the lid, turn up the flame, add the rice in the pan, and deglaze with white wine
  10. Keep stirring for about 3 mins to make sure the rice doesn't stick to the pan and burn
  11. Flood the mix with broth!
  12. Lower the flame, relax, and start sipping a glass of white wine...if you didn't already
  13. Cut out the core of the apple, then cut it quarters and chop them in thin slices of about 3 mm
  14. Keep stirring the rice to prevent it to stick to the pan and burn, add broth when dry
  15. After about 30 mins cooking, start tasting the rice
  16. When the rice is soft, turn down the fire
  17. Add the mix of gorgonzola, and stir well
  18. Add apple, raisin and nuts. Stir again, and seal with a lead
  19. Wait 5 mins and serve the risotto, and add a bit of black pepper if you like it

Enjoy!
